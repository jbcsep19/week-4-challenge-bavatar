Java Composition

28 July 2017 
Composition is process of building complex objects from simple ones. This type of relationship is a containing relationship.
It uses OO principles to achieve greater simplicity, maintainability and re-use of code.
Main benefits of composition
 
You can find such relationships quite regularly in the real world. A car, for example, has an engine and modern coffee
machines often have an integrated grinder and a brewing unit.
